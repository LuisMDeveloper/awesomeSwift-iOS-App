Description
Official iOS app of the collaborative list of awesome swift resources:
a curated list of awesome Swift frameworks, libraries, and software for iOS / OSX / tvOS / watchOS and Linux.

Feel free to contribute on github: https://github.com/matteocrippa/awesome-swift

App for mobile and tablet (universal) supports:

- push notification for new repositories
- 3d force touch to easily preview a repository
